Fighting Boredom

Sometimes all you need to be entertained is to pretend that floor is lava. And sometimes you need an amazing list from a cool website*.

*This is that website

Geocaching

Geocaching

Geocaching is basically a global scavenger hunt. People around the globe hide little containers called ‘geocaches’ and mark the location on the app using GPS. Other people can see the location of these hidden ‘treasures’ but do not know what they look like or where exactly are they hidden. Once you find one, you write down your name and re-hide it for the next person to find. The app is free to use and you would be surprised how many in your neighbourhood are using it. Sanitise your hands and happy caching!

HouseParty

HouseParty

Recently video chatting has become the normality for me and as much as I love discussing my meals in detail, I prefer to change things up once in a while. HouseParty is an app that combines group chats, video calls and playing games. And even though they only have a handful of games they are certain to cause few laughs or at least pass some time.

Stargazing

Stargazing

Even if stargazing has never been your thing, now is the time to start! You don’t have to be a professional to spot some delicious space events happening in the next few months. Both June and July have lunar eclipses and June is also lucky enough to host a partial solar eclipse. Mid July is the best time to find Jupiter and Saturn as they will be at the closest point to Earth while August 12 - 13 is when Perseid Metero Shower will be visible. All you need is your phone and curiosity.

Catch up on TV

Catch up on TV

Finally that day has come where it’s socially acceptable, nay, even encouraged to stay at home and watch some TV. Whether it’s Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+ or good, old-fashioned TV, now is the time to binge all movies and TV series everyone is always talking about. And even if you don’t have said streaming services, explore YouTube, I can’t even count how many hours have been lost in that rabbit hole.

Explore Mars

Explore Mars

Do you have what it takes to be the next Mars Rover driver? You can test your driving as well as logical reasoning skills by playing these fun little games NASA has created. If driving is not your thing, explore the 360o view of Mars here. (https://accessmars.withgoogle.com/)

Learn a Tik Tok Dance

Learn a Tik Tok Dance

As funny as it sounds, there is an abundance of dance tutorials available online to prepare you for your 15-30s TikTok spotlight. These dances might be short, but if you’re anything like me, few hours will pass by without even blinking, you’ll have learned some sweet moves and exercised without even noticing.

Pick up a new Skill

Pick up a new Skill

Have you ever wondered if a certain job or hobby is for you? Well now you have time to find out. Pick up a new language, see if you have an artistic bone in your body, brush up on your singing skills or browse through countless free online courses. Few of my favourite websites are edx.org, udemy.com, masterclass.com and skillshare.com.

In a time where everything is uncertain and basic daily joys of life have become harder to come by, I have created this website to use as a guide for passing the extra time we have and find new and unique ways of entertainment. Stay safe, stay at home and explore :)
