Good News Stories

In case cute animals are not enough, I’ve chosen my favourite inspiring, feel-good, ‘faith in humanity restored’ type of stories to fill you with good vibes.

(Click on images to find out more)

Some Good News

John Krasinski is hosting a YouTube video diaries called “Some Good News” where he posts weekly videos of good news stories during this pandemic.

War Veteran Raises €30m

Captain Moore, a 99-year-old war veteran, has completed 100 laps around his yard and raised £29m for NHS. He originally set out to raise £1000 which he reached in 24h.

Lost Himalayas

According to locals in Punjab, India, Himalayas are visible for the first time in years. It is predicted that pollution in this region has dropped by 44%.

5km Run For Our Heros

A new trend has become increasingly more popular - run 5km, donate €5 and challenge 5 friends to do the same. A great way to support our health care workers and stay active.

Rainbow Drawings

If you’ve noticed an increasing amount of rainbow drawings being placed in windows, then you have witnessed a movement meant to spread hope and show support.

F1 To The Rescue

Mercedes F1 team has teamed up with University College London and created a breathing aid for coronavirus patients which has reduced the need for ventilators.
